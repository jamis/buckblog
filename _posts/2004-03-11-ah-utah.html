Ah, Utah. I woke up this morning to a beautiful day—the sky was blue, Mount Timpanogos was towering majestically and snow-capped to the north, and it appeared as though spring might truly be here despite it being only 35 degrees outside.

Then, I turn on the radio as I’m driving to work, and I catch the tail end of the weather report.

They’re expecting more snow this weekend.

Bah. Humbug.
