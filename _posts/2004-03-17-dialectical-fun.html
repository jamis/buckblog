My friend Danny Amelang pointed me to a great site: The Dialectizer. “What does it do?” you ask? Ah. :) I’d hoped you would…

The Dialectizer, through some brilliant software wizardry, will convert any text (in English) into a dialect of English that you specify. The available dialects include “redneck”, “jive”, and “swedesh chef”, just to give you an idea of what’s possible.

Curious? Try this link for a taste; it’s my blog in redneck.
