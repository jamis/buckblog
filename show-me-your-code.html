How this usually works:

  1. We shake hands and swap contracts, NDA's, and so forth.
  2. You share your screen remotely, using whatever tool you're familiar with. Invite whomever you like to the review.
  3. You walk me through your code, and we discuss it in realtime. I'll point out potential issues, and suggest possible methods of improvement.
  4. You send payment. PayPal works well, but I'm flexible. We can negotiate other payment methods.
  5. At the very end, I send you a summary of what we discussed and what was suggested.

If the stars align, it can all be done in a day, but please allow a few days to make room for scheduling.
